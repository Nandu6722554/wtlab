vision & mission

Vision of the Institute To be a state of the art institution of engineering in pursuit of excellence, in the service of society. Mission of the Institute To excel in providing quality education at under graduate and graduate levels. To encourage research and innovation. To provide infrastructure and facilities to meet the latest technological needs. To establish Centres of Excellence through active interaction with industry. To nurture students towards holistic development with human values and ethics.
